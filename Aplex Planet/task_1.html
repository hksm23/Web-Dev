Endangered Species

List of Endangered Species in India

India's Red List of 2018 was released at the Rio+20 Earth Summit, and since then, new animals have been added yearly. As of the 2023-1 update from the IUCN Red List, over 950 species of animals are listed as critically endangered, endangered, or vulnerable. This includes 481 animals listed as vulnerable. The list is a call to action for world leaders and conservationists to address the extinction threat and protect these species.
